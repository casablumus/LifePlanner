Difficulty guide

How each level is defined. Adjust individual meal difficulties in All Meals.

Easy≤ 30 min · minimal technique
Simple prep, mostly shelf-stable or fresh ingredients. Good for tired weeknights.
Medium30–60 min · some multitasking
Multiple components at once — protein, sauce, side. Some technique involved. Usually needs thawing.
Involved60+ min · plan ahead
Long cook times, complex prep, or precise multi-dish timing. Best for relaxed evenings or weekends.
⚠Thaw flag: marked when the main protein needs to come out of the freezer the night before.
